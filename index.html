ABOUT WEDDING DRONE NEW YORK

We film weddings from the sky. It's that simple. Wedding Drone New York produces bespoke wedding movies with breathtaking aerial cinematography.

WHAT WE DO

We are a full-service production company and aerial videography team, creating awe-inspiring wedding films. Wedding Drone New York works intimately with our couples to create the wedding movie of their dreams. On your wedding day, our team films all outdoor events from the sky. When the party moves indoors, our crew switches gears and grabs state-of-the-art HD video cameras, documenting wedding festivities throughout the night.

WHAT TO EXPECT

As you head off on your honeymoon, Wedding Drone New York heads into post-production. We edit audio from your vows to the music of your choice, combined with stunning visual footage, to create a wedding video like no other. The end result is a deeply-moving cinematic document of your wedding day.

THE CINEMATOGRAPHER

Founder Cody Raisig is a world-renowned photographer and cinematographer. He began his career as a principal cinematographer, specializing in extreme skiing. Raisig traveled the globe for four winters, documenting the sport's most high-profile athletes from Switzerland to Japan and British Columbia. Growing tired of the mountain lifestyle, Raisig settled down in New York City, establishing himself as a wedding photographer for Manhattan's elite in 2009. With 200 weddings under his belt and the success of Cody Raisig Photography, Wedding Drone New York's astonishing visuals are rooted in experience, care and respect for a wedding's magnitude.
